Story

This is a very short story that I wrote for a 53 word story contest a long time ago

The dim orange of the setting sun slowly gives way to the light gray of dusk.
Trees sway in the gentle breeze of a warm summer night.
As the sun dips below the horizon, the summertime serenity is quickly
interrupted by the mournful cry of whatever creatures of shadow lurk within the trees.
